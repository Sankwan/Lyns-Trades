Trading Psychology

Trading is more than just charts and indicators; it's about comprehending the psychology behind market movements. This scales down to the mental and emotional aspects of trading that can significantly impact success.

I firmly believe that trading comprises 80% psychology and 20% strategy. For this reason, my course emphasizes not only trading strategies but also the development of a resilient trading mindset.

Risk Management

Learn the art of risk management and adhere to the stringent rules I've developed to ensure your long-term trading success.

This private mentorship program also includes daily trade ideas and signals to facilitate the growth of your trading account while you continue to learn.

Risk management is the process of minimizing potential losses while maximizing potential gains. Key elements include:

  • Position Sizing: Determine the size of each trade based on your risk tolerance and the distance to your stop-loss order.
  • Capital Preservation: Protecting your trading capital is paramount. Never risk more than you can afford to lose in a single trade.
